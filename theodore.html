Théodore de Montaigu

A fiercely devoted French revolutionary, Théodore has stumbled into the modern world - and he’s not impressed. He greets you like a knight, politely disturbed by your “magic lanterns” (light bulbs). He quickly loses patience and calls you an “infamous scoundrel” when you cross his path.

Tool/accessory: Old fountain pen

Number of chapters: 3
